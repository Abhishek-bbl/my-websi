📸 Unseen, Yet Forever Framed

🖼️ If I had a photo of that moment you leaned on my shoulder, I’d frame it forever. But I don’t need one — my heart remembers it perfectly.

🖼️ No lens captured your smile as you fed me that first bite of biryani... but it lives in my mind, replaying like a favorite film scene.

🖼️ That look you gave me when I handed you the water bottle — the one you thought I didn’t buy — no camera could’ve caught the sparkle in your eyes.

🖼️ I wish there was a selfie of us from the garden — me with my arm around you, your head on my shoulder... but some pictures are meant only for the soul.

Even without a single photo together, I remember everything. Because love doesn’t need proof — only feeling. And with you, I felt everything. 💖
